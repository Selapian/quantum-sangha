Quantum Gong is the first Quantum Mindfulness Bell, derived from the practice of Thich Nhat Hanh, which rings at Quantum Random times (about once every 40 minutes), using a free Quantum Random Number Generator (QRNG). The QRNG pushes the same "True Random" Gong Sound, all at the same time, to everyone connected to the app. When we each hear the Gong Sound, we all practice Mindfulness, or sit and meditate, to cultivate ourselves!

Because websites cannot push notifications while the screen is closed, it is necessary to install "Quantum Gong" as an Android App, or, if on iOS (Apple), press the three dots, then Press "Share", and scroll down to "Add to Home Screen." Then Open 'Quantum Gong' like a native app from your Home Screen. If you are on Desktop, please use Google Chrome, install the App, then open "Quantum Gong" from the Desktop.
